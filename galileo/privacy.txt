GALILEO PRIVACY POLICY

GENERAL INFORMATION
Galileo is software built by TechOctave, LLC to help build a Targeted & Relevant Twitter Following. Any questions about this Privacy Policy should be addressed to support@techoctave.com.

INFORMATION GATHERING AND USAGE
Galileo does not gather or store your information in any form.

COOKIES
Galileo does not use cookies.

DATA STORAGE
Galileo stores your Consumer Key and Consumer Secret locally only to the extent that it serves to provide ease of access to your Twitter account. This information is never stored on our servers or transmitted anywhere. The only communication is between your encrypted browser and Twitter's OAuth authentication process. This data is expunged from your local browser on signing out.

DISCLOSURE
Galileo does not collect personally identifiable information under any circumstance, so there is no data to disclose.

For complaints that cannot be resolved, and consistent with the Safe Harbor Enforcement Principle, we have committed to cooperate with data protection authorities located within Switzerland or the European Union (or their authorized representatives).
